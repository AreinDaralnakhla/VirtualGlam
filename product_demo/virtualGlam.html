How to Use VirtualGlam

1. Capture your photo by clicking the Capture Photo button or by showing a point gesture to the camera.

2. You can also upload your photo by clicking the Choose File button.

3. Select the garment you want to try on.

4. Click on the Try-On button to generate the output try-on image.

To get the best results consider uploading a high-quality image of yourself preferably of size 768x1024 pixels
